Portishead singer Beth Gibbons & Rustin Man, aka Paul Webb of Talk Talk fame, produce music that's best described as quiet, acoustic folk and jazz balladry which showcases Gibbons's versatile voice. They have released one album, Out of Season, which spawned two music videos: "Tom the Model" and "Mysteries".
